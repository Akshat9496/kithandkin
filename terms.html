Section 2

Nature of Service

Kith & Kin is a companionship and concierge service — not a medical, nursing, or healthcare provider. We observe, report, coordinate, and accompany. We do not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or provide clinical care of any kind.

Our services include structured home visits, vital sign observation (BP and pulse), medication adherence checks (confirming medications are taken as scheduled), wellbeing conversations, errand coordination, and family reporting.

In the event of a medical emergency, our associates will notify the client family immediately and coordinate with the elder's designated doctor or hospital. We are not a substitute for emergency medical services.
