What We Do

A concierge service built on
trust, not technology.

I

Structured Home Visits

Regular, scheduled in-person visits to your parent's home. Consistent, never missed, personally supervised.

II

Health Monitoring

BP and pulse checked and logged at every visit. Medication adherence observed. Early warning signs identified and flagged.

III

Family Kept Informed

Same-day WhatsApp report after every visit — vitals, medication, mood, and any issues. You know everything.

Errands & Coordination

Bills, groceries, home repairs, banking, hospital appointments — we coordinate so nothing falls through the cracks.

Before You Begin

Your Questions, Answered

We know you're trusting us with the person who matters most. These are the questions every family asks — and deserves a straight answer to.

Who Visits

Who will visit my parents?

Visits are by trained, verified associates supervised by the founder. The founder personally conducts every onboarding visit and makes a monthly check-in with every client.

Trust

How do I know the person visiting is trustworthy?

Every associate undergoes police verification and structured training. The founder's background and references are available on request. The first visit is always by the founder personally.

Updates

How will I know what happened during each visit?

On the same day as every visit, your family WhatsApp group receives a structured report: vitals, medication, mood observations, and issues flagged. Signature and Premiere members also get updates between visits.

Emergencies

What if my parent has a medical emergency?

We are the first call. We notify your family immediately, coordinate with your doctor or hospital, and stay on-site until stable. Premiere members receive a written emergency plan at onboarding.

Pricing

How much does it cost? Is there a free trial?

Plans start from ₹5,999 per month. Payment is collected before your first visit. No free trials — but the founder's onboarding visit is included in your first month so you see exactly how we work from day one.

Health Check

How does the annual health check work?

We coordinate a comprehensive home collection covering 80+ health parameters. The founder is present and the report is shared with the family with a plain-language summary. Included after 6 months on Signature, 3 months on Premiere.

Getting Started

How quickly can we start? Is there a contract?

Onboarding takes 5–7 days from confirmed booking. No long-term contract. Cancel anytime with 7 days notice before your next billing date. We raise your invoice 7 days before each month begins. We'd rather earn your loyalty than lock it in.

Check-in Call

What happens on the weekly check-in call?

Every week we call your parent for a structured conversation — how they're feeling, whether any needs have come up, anything the family should know. It's not a medical call. It's a consistent, caring presence that catches small issues before they become big ones.
